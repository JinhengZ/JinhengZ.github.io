Garden Design
This application enables users to meticulously design their gardens prior to
the actual landscaping. It offers a user-friendly interface where users can seamlessly
drag and drop various plant elements into a virtual garden space. The application provides
comprehensive recommendations based on the specific characteristics of each plant,
such as moisture requirements, sunlight needs, and associated costs. Additionally,
it features an automated tool to calculate the total cost of the garden design,
aiding customers in budget planning and decision-making.

Student Loan Calculator

This application is designed for students who have taken out loans from their
universities and seek to manage their finances effectively. Within this app,
users have the flexibility to input their initial loan amount, interest rate,
and desired repayment amount. The StudentLoanCalculator then automatically
calculates the total repayment amount required, updating this information
in real time. Additionally, it provides valuable insights, such as the total
savings achieved compared to the standard repayment plan, and presents a graph
to illustrate the potential additional costs incurred if the payment deadline is missed.

BreathOfWind
This project is developed to assist users in enhancing their lung capacity and breathing techniques. Utilizing advanced Virtual Reality (VR) technology, it tracks the speed and strength of the user's breath. The system employs a sophisticated breathing model display that guides users in adjusting their breathing pattern to achieve optimal performance. A visual feedback mechanism is integrated into the program, where a green bubble indicates successful breathing techniques, while a red bubble suggests the need for further practice and improvement.
